[h1] Calamity Entropy Mod [/h1]

This mod is an expansion for the Calamity Mod, aiming to add more weapons and items to the Calamity progression, as well as new gameplay content.

-This mod adds-

Four new bosses

Unique weapons (including new weapon series for Warrior, Mage, and Rogue, as well as even more unique whips)

A large number of new accessories

A new boss health bar style

A new armor reforging mechanic (optional)

New pets

Vanity sets for the developers and their friends

The Golden Genesis Toilet, which can be sat on or used to ascend

The Void Toilet

The Hallowed Genesis Monolith

More new content will be added continuously in the future.
